Miso Yaki Ongiri

Category: Japanese, Vegeterian / Author : Zizi-cook / Date:July 13, 2024

Ingredients

  • 2 Tsp Miso
  • 1 Tsp of sugar
  • 1 Tsp of marini
  • 3 cups of cooked Japanese short-grain rice.
  • 10- 12 shiso leaves (optional)
  • 2 tsp of roasted white sesemi seeds salt
  • 1/2 tsp of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse and cook 2 cups of sushi rice, then mix it with 1/3 cup rice vinegar, 3 tablespoons sugar, and 1 teaspoon salt, and let it cool.
  2. Slice fresh fish, cucumber, avocado, and other desired fillings into thin strips.
  3. Place a bamboo sushi mat covered with plastic wrap on a flat surface, then put a sheet of nori shiny side down on the mat.
  4. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, add fillings in the center, and use the mat to roll the sushi tightly, sealing the edge with water.
